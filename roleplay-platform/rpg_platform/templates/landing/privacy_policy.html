This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe experience on our platform.

RoleplayHub ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.   • Account Information: When you register, we collect your username, email address, password, and any optional profile information you provide.
  • Content: Information you create, post, or share on our platform, including character profiles, messages, and other content.
  • Communications: Your communications with us and other users, including feedback, support requests, and messages.
  • Preferences: Your settings, preferences, and consent selections.

2.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Device Information: Information about your device, such as IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers.
  • Usage Information: Information about how you use our platform, including visited pages, time spent, and interactions.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use cookies and similar technologies to collect information about your browsing behavior and preferences.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our platform

  • To personalize your experience and provide content and features relevant to your interests
  • To monitor and analyze usage and trends to improve the platform
  • To detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions and unauthorized access
  • To comply with legal obligations

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Other Users: Information you choose to share publicly or with specific users will be visible to those users.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section below.

Our platform is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 18, we will promptly take steps to delete that information.
